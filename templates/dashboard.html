Life Motor Insurance

Description

Covers damages or losses related to the insured vehicle.

Options

Three types of policies: First-Party, Second-Party, and Third-Party insurance.

  • First-Party Insurance: Covers the person insuring their own motor vehicle.
  • Second-Party Insurance: Covers the person involved in an accident with the insured.
  • Third-Party Insurance: Covers the damage or injury to third-party property or individuals.
